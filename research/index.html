Current Research

I'm a math graduate student at the University of Oregon studying algebraic geometry under the guidance of Nicolas Addington. My work focuses on computing Hasse-Weil zeta functions of smooth hypersurfaces and complete intersections in Grassmannians using $p$-adic cohomology.

My code for these computations is available on GitHub.

Past Work

A few years ago I was interested in computing blowups of toric varieties. Some of the code is available here.

I got my BS in math and physics at the University of Washington in Seattle in 2018. I wrote my senior honors thesis on repeated dimensions of irreducible representations of compact semisimple Lie algebras, under the guidance of Monty McGovern.

As an undergrad at UW I worked in Prof. David Cobden's graphene lab for a summer. Before that, I did some work on SageMathCloud (now CoCalc). I contributed code to this repository.

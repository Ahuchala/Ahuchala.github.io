Current Research

I'm a math graduate student at the University of Oregon studying algebraic geometry under the guidance of Nicolas Addington. My work focuses on computing Hasse-Weil zeta functions of smooth hypersurfaces and complete intersections in Grassmannians.

My code for these computations is available on GitHub.

Past Work

I previously studied mathematics and physics at the University of Washington. I wrote my senior thesis on repeated dimensions of irreducible representations of compact semisimple Lie algebras, under the guidance of Monty McGovern.

I'm a math grad student at UO University of Oregon studying algebraic geometry under Nicolas Addington. I'm interested in computing Hasse-Weil zeta functions of smooth hypersurfaces and complete intersections in Grassmannians. Code is available here. I previously studied mathematics and physics at the University of Washington. I wrote my senior thesis on repeated dimensions of irreducible representations of compact semisimple Lie algebras, under the guidance of Monty McGovern.
